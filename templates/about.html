About Dou Dizhu

Dou Dizhu, often translated as "Fight the Landlord," is a popular Chinese card game that involves three players, with one taking on the role of the 'landlord' against the other two players who are 'peasants'. The game is celebrated for its strategic complexity and has various layers of rules regarding card combinations, bidding for the role of the landlord, and dynamic gameplay that challenges both the mind and the spirit of cooperation among players.

Do you know?

Dou Dizhu originated in Hubei province during the late 1980s.


The game quickly became popular across China as a competitive and strategic card game.


Today, Dou Dizhu is enjoyed in numerous online platforms and competitions worldwide.


Annual championships and tournaments highlight the game's ongoing appeal and competitive nature.
